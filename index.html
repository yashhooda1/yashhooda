⚡ About Me

I'm a 24-year-old Data Engineer with a deep passion for intelligent systems that solve real-world challenges. I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Texas at Dallas, along with professional certifications in IBM AI Engineering, IBM Data Science, and the Databricks Certified Data Engineer Associate.

My core expertise lies in Data Engineering with a strong focus on building scalable, efficient data platforms that transform complex datasets into actionable insights. I specialize in designing and optimizing end-to-end data pipelines, working extensively with modern data stack technologies including Microsoft Fabric, Databricks, PySpark, SQL, and distributed data processing systems.

My work experience spans data modeling, ETL/ELT pipeline development, and large-scale data transformation, with a focus on performance optimization, reliability, and cost efficiency. I am passionate about enabling data-driven decision-making by building robust data infrastructure that supports analytics, reporting, and advanced use cases.

In addition to my data engineering expertise, I have a strong foundation in artificial intelligence and machine learning. I actively explore applications of AI, including NLP and LLMs, to enhance data workflows and unlock new capabilities within modern data ecosystems. I thrive on building innovative, AI-powered solutions and am continually exploring the frontiers of artificial intelligence and its applications across various industries. My ultimate goal is to harness AI to deliver impactful, real-world solutions that improve lives.

I'm also highly proficient with today's most advanced AI tools, including ChatGPT-4o, Gemini 2.5 Flash, Grok-3, Microsoft CoPilot, Claude Sonnet 4, Perplexity, DeepSeek R1, and Meta AI Llama 4 Maverick. I actively experiment with these platforms to build smarter, more adaptive systems and stay ahead in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

Beyond tech, I live an active and curious life. I'm an avid runner with a love for aviation, astronomy, hiking, and travel. I also enjoy unwinding with Netflix or documentaries, and I value spending time with family and friends. In my free time, I'm often reading about the latest breakthroughs in technology, AI, and the future of intelligent systems.
